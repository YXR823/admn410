About Us

Our pet company, founded by Milo Masson in 2010, was an early company selling pet food and household goods. Since its
inception in 2010, the company has grown from five to more than 100 people, and the company has started to expand into
a variety of businesses. So far, our company has developed a wedding, beauty and breeding program, which is an alternative
pet shop. The location we choose is near the residential area with heavy traffic, and the supplier is ourselves. Our
company is originally a pet store.
